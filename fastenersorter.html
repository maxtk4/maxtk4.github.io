Fastener Sorter

This fastener sorter was designed with a team of four engineering students at Rice University. I designed most of the mechanical and electrical systems and integration tested with the computer vision software, along with writing software to control the various electromechanical systems. It used stepper and servo motors controlled by a Raspberry Pi 3B running a custom computer vision algorithm written in Python and OpenCV.

Through iterative design and tweaking to the camera, conveyer belt, and computer vision system, we were able to achieve over 80% accuracy sorting between different types of mechanical fasteners.
