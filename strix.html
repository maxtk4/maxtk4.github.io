Strix

Strix is a custom flight computer I designed from scratch around an STM32L476 chip. It is designed to replace and improve upon to features available on commercial rocket flight controllers, and is equipped with accelerometers, gyroscopes, a magnetometer, and a barometric altimeter to accurately estimate position.

I designed Strix using KiCAD, and programmed using a combination of STM32CubeMX and STM32CubeIDE. The software is written in C, and is built on the hardware abstraction layer provided by ST Microeletronics to simplify the hardware interfacing. Below are some images of the routing and a 3d render of the PCB in KiCAD.

I tested the power supply stability with various input voltages and an oscilloscope, and confirmed that the board succesfully functions. The software was tested to record data from a flight and print it out when connected to a computer, and is able to fire pyrotechnics on any of the channels.
